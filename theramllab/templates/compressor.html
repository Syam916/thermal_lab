PROCEDURE:

The pressure inside the air tank is first made atmospheric. This is done by opening 
the outlet valve of the air tank and letting the air to atmosphere. The readings 
that are obtained give the values above atmospheric and hence to be added with 
1.033 kgf/cm2.
Start the motor. Adjust the pressure to a particular value. Note the time taken
for five revolutions of the energy meter. Then calculate the input to the compressor
assuming a motor efficiency of 85%. Note the manometer reading and find the actual
quantity of air drawn into the compressor. The experiment in repeated for different pressures.
